# Slimes and Magma Cubes

## Classification

Simple family. Both Slime and MagmaCube count under the same family for friendly-fire (they don't damage each other). Squad-eligible but they don't form natural squads with each other - usually they get the tactic and act solo.

## Tactics

| Tactic | Threshold | Effect |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| **Slime Surge** | diff >=0.50 | Gets Speed II (4 sec) to surge toward the player in a burst. Triggers a tactical search-effect at its position. |

## Notes
- Magma Cubes get the same surge behavior since they extend Slime.
- Vanilla slime split-on-death behavior is unchanged; smaller slimes can also receive the tactic at the same threshold.
- Slimes are not smart-family so they don't kite or break LOS - they just surge straight at you faster than vanilla.
