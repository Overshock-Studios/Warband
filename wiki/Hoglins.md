# Hoglins and Zoglins

## Classification

Simple family. They commit, follow the squad target, regroup, call backup. Squad-eligible. Default role: Bruiser.

## Tactics

| Tactic | Threshold | Effect |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| **Hoglin Stampede** | diff >=0.45 | When engaged in a squad, gets Speed I (5 sec) and Strength I (5 sec) to crash into the line. Signals tactical effect. |
| **Leap Unreachable** | diff >=0.65 | Will leap onto raised perches. |
| **Mob Stack Climb** | diff >=0.65 | Squad members will stack on each other to scale obstacles. |

## Family
Hoglins and Zoglins share friendly-fire and squad recruitment as one family.

## Squad behavior
As simple-family they don't kite or break LOS - they full-commit. The stampede tactic is what makes a hoglin squad genuinely scary: by the time you've heard the roar, they're already at +Speed and +Strength bearing down on you.
